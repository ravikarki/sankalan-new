DIGITAL INDIA

Mission:

Make government services available to citizens electronically. How?
  1. Improve online infrastructure
  2. Increase Internet connectivity
Launched: 1 July 2015 by PM Narendra Modi

3 Main components:

  1. Creation of digital infrastructure
  2. Deliver services digitally
  3. Digital literacy

Slogan: Power to empower


Who monitors the implementation of the scheme?

Digital India advisory group, chaired by Ministry of Communications and IT

First main step:

    Bharat Broadband Network Limited is the custodian of this project
    BBNL has asked United Telecoms Limited to connect 250,000 villages and provide FTTH based broadband
    Expected to be completed by 2017

9 Pillars of Digital India:

  1. Broadband Highways
  2. Universal Access to Mobile Connectivity
  3. Public Internet Access Programme
  4. e-Governance
  5. e-Kranti
  6. Information for all
  7. Electronics Manufacturing
  8. IT for jobs
  9. Early Harvest Programmes

3 Main Services started:

  1. DigiLocker
  2. Attendance.gov.in
  3. MyGov.in

Various Initiatives:

  1. Observing Digital India Week
  2. Global tech giants Facebook's CEO, Mark Zuckerberg, has promised to work on WiFi Hotspots in rural India Google committed to provide broadband connectivity on 500 railway stations in India.

Digital India

Government of India has launch the “Digital India” campaign to develop a digitally empowered society and to digitally integrate the government departments and the citizens of India. It aims at ensuring the government services are made available to people of India electronically. It seeks to lay emphasis on e-governance and transform India into a digitally empowered society. The Digital India initiative is a promising initiative of the Indian Government.

Turn Coat

"Get ready to drive through a battle of thoughts with your own self on a buzzer press."

    Team Size: 1
    No of Rounds: 3
Round 1
Each participant will be given 2 minutes to speak on a debatable topic, with alternative durations of 30 seconds each in favor or against the issue.

Round 2
Two participants will be given the same topic to speak. Alternatively, they will have to speak in favor and against.

Round 3
All qualifying participants will be given the same topic to speak. They would randomly be asked to present their views in contradiction to the previous speaker.
